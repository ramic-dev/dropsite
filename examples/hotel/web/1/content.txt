Nestled in the rolling hills of the Cotswolds, The Grand Oak has welcomed guests since 1890. Our 24-room boutique hotel blends period character with modern comfort.

Wake to birdsong and countryside views. Spend your days exploring gardens, walking ancient trails or simply unwinding by the fire with a good book.

Our restaurant serves seasonal British cuisine using ingredients from our kitchen garden and local farms. The spa offers a range of treatments designed to restore and rejuvenate.
