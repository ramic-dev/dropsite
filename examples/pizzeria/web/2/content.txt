Our menu celebrates the simplicity of true Neapolitan cooking.

Margherita, Marinara, Diavola, Quattro Formaggi, Prosciutto e Rucola, Burrata e Pomodorini. Each pizza is stretched by hand and baked to order.

We also serve bruschetta, arancini, insalata caprese, tiramisù and panna cotta. Our wine list features a curated selection from Campania and Sicily.
