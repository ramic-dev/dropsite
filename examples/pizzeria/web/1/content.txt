Bella Napoli has been serving authentic Neapolitan pizza since 1985. What started as a small family kitchen has grown into a beloved neighborhood landmark.

Every pizza is made with imported Italian flour, San Marzano tomatoes and fresh mozzarella. Our wood-fired oven reaches 450°C, giving each pie the perfect charred crust in just 90 seconds.

We believe great food brings people together. Whether you are here for a quick lunch or a long family dinner, you are always welcome at our table.
